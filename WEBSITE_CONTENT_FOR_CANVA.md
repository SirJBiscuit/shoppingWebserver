## FAQ Section

### Section Title
**"Frequently Asked Questions"**

**Q: Is Listly really free?**  
A: Yes! Listly is free forever with unlimited lists and core features. Premium unlocks advanced features.

**Q: Can I try Premium before buying?**  
A: Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial of Premium with no credit card required.

**Q: Does it work offline?**  
A: Absolutely! Listly works completely offline and syncs when you're back online.

**Q: Can I share lists with family?**  
A: Yes! Share lists with anyone, and Premium users get real-time collaboration.

**Q: What platforms does Listly support?**  
A: Listly works on iOS, Android, and web browsers. It's also a PWA you can install.

**Q: How do I cancel Premium?**  
A: Cancel anytime from your account settings. No questions asked, no fees.

**Q: Is my data secure?**  
A: Yes! We use bank-level encryption and never sell your data. Privacy first.

**Q: Can I export my data?**  
A: Yes! Export your lists, recipes, and data anytime in multiple formats.
