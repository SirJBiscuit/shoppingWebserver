## Name Analysis

### **What Makes a Good App Name:**
1. ✅ Easy to spell
2. ✅ Easy to pronounce
3. ✅ Memorable
4. ✅ Explains what it does
5. ✅ Available domain
6. ✅ Not trademarked
7. ✅ Sounds professional
8. ✅ Works internationally

### **Avoid:**
- ❌ Too long (3+ words)
- ❌ Hard to spell
- ❌ Confusing pronunciation
- ❌ Already taken
- ❌ Too generic
- ❌ Negative connotations

- Namecheap.com - Domain search

## My Final Recommendation

### **🏆 Go with "ShopSmart"**

**Reasons:**
1. **Clear & Descriptive** - People instantly know what it does
2. **Easy to Remember** - Simple, two-word compound
3. **Professional** - Sounds like a real company
4. **SEO-Friendly** - "Shop" and "Smart" are searchable terms
5. **Brandable** - Easy to create marketing around
6. **International** - Works in English-speaking markets
7. **Positive** - Implies intelligence and savings
