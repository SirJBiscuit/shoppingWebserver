## 🎨 Visual Identity

### **Logo Concept:**
- Clean, modern checkmark or list icon
- Simple wordmark "Listly"
- Can work as icon alone or with text

### **Color Palette:**

**Primary Colors:**
- **Primary Blue:** `#3B82F6` (rgb(59, 130, 246))
  - Trust, reliability, calm
  - Use for: Primary buttons, links, active states

- **Primary Purple:** `#8B5CF6` (rgb(139, 92, 246))
  - Innovation, creativity
  - Use for: Accents, premium features, gradients

**Secondary Colors:**
- **Success Green:** `#10B981` (rgb(16, 185, 129))
  - Savings, completion, positive actions
  
- **Warning Orange:** `#F59E0B` (rgb(245, 158, 11))
  - Alerts, expiring items, attention needed
  
- **Error Red:** `#EF4444` (rgb(239, 68, 68))
  - Errors, deletions, critical alerts

**Neutral Colors:**
- **Gray 900:** `#111827` (Dark mode background)
- **Gray 800:** `#1F2937` (Dark mode cards)
- **Gray 100:** `#F3F4F6` (Light mode background)
- **White:** `#FFFFFF` (Light mode cards)

### **Typography:**

**Primary Font:** Inter (or system default)
- Clean, modern, highly readable
- Works great for UI and body text

**Headings:**
- Font weight: 700 (Bold)
- Letter spacing: -0.02em (tight)

**Body:**
- Font weight: 400 (Regular)
- Line height: 1.5

**Small text:**
- Font weight: 500 (Medium)
- Opacity: 0.8

## 💬 Voice & Tone

### **Brand Voice:**
- **Friendly** - Like a helpful friend, not a corporate robot
- **Clear** - No jargon, simple language
- **Encouraging** - Positive, supportive, motivating
- **Smart** - Intelligent but not condescending

### **Writing Style:**
- Use contractions (you're, we'll, it's)
- Keep sentences short and punchy
- Use active voice
- Avoid technical jargon
- Be conversational

### **Examples:**

**Good:**
- "You're all set! 🎉"
- "Let's add some items to your list"
- "Save $20 this week with coupons"

**Bad:**
- "Configuration complete."
- "Please proceed to add items to your shopping list"
- "Potential savings: $20.00 USD"

---

## 📱 App Naming Conventions

### **Features:**
- Keep names simple and descriptive
- Use action verbs when possible
- Avoid technical terms

**Examples:**
- ✅ "Shopping Lists" not "List Management"
- ✅ "Meal Planner" not "Meal Planning Module"
- ✅ "Pantry" not "Inventory Tracker"

### **Buttons:**
- Use clear action words
- Keep it short (1-3 words)

**Examples:**
- ✅ "Add Item"
- ✅ "Save"
- ✅ "Share List"
- ❌ "Submit"
- ❌ "OK"
