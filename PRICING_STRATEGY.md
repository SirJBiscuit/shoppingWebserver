## 📱 Distribution Strategy

### **Phase 1: Web App (Current)**
- **Platform:** shop.cloudmc.online
- **Cost:** $0 (no app store fees)
- **Revenue:** Keep 97% (PayPal 3% fee)
- **Benefits:**
  - Instant updates
  - No approval process
  - Full control
  - Cross-platform

### **Phase 2: PWA (Progressive Web App)**
- **Platform:** Installable from browser
- **Cost:** $0 (no app store fees)
- **Benefits:**
  - Works like native app
  - Offline support
  - Push notifications
  - Home screen icon

### **Phase 3: Native Apps (Future)**
- **Platform:** App Store & Google Play
- **Cost:** 30% fee (15% after year 1)
- **When:** After 1,000+ users
- **Benefits:**
  - More discovery
  - App store credibility
  - 3x more users
  - Worth the 30% fee

## 🚫 What We DON'T Limit

**Never limit these (keeps users happy):**
- ❌ Number of lists
- ❌ Basic icons
- ❌ Animations
- ❌ Export to CSV/PDF
- ❌ Dark mode
- ❌ Mobile responsiveness
- ❌ Sharing via link
- ❌ Core features

**Why?** Users will feel nickel-and-dimed and leave. We want happy free users who become paying customers.

---

## ✅ What We Gate Behind Premium

**Gate these (clear value add):**
- ✅ AI features (costs money to run)
- ✅ Delivery integration (premium convenience)
- ✅ Coupons (saves them money)
- ✅ Real-time collaboration (server costs)
- ✅ Advanced analytics (power user feature)
- ✅ Voice assistant (premium experience)
- ✅ Cloud backup (storage costs)

**Why?** These provide clear ROI and justify the subscription cost.
